What type of Religon is Endology?


Endology
Endology is a religion, also referred to as an Occult. We believe in the supernatural and the spiritual. It is important to note that a cult and an occult are not the same. A cult typically refers to a group with extreme or unorthodox beliefs, often led by a charismatic leader, whereas an occult involves the study and practice of mystical, supernatural, or magical phenomena.
